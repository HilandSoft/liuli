Cash payday loan cost and fees

Finance Cost
Credit charges (administration fees) are 1.333% per day of the credit advanced over the repayment period of time.

Term of Credit
The term of the credit is maximum two months or three pay periods (whichever is the sooner).

- Short Term Cash Solution Only
- Up to $1,500 over night. First-time borrowers can get up to a maximum $500.

Extension Fee
The extension fee is the finance cost of the current principle balance.

- Extension must be submitted not less that 2 business days before due date.
- Extension fee is due on original installment payment due date.
