Applying for cash loans online

Who can apply?

Qualifying is easy. There are no credit checks and you just need to meet the following minimum requirements:

  • Over 18 years old and an Australian citizen or permanent resident [Loans are not available to residents of Queenslant (QLD, New South Wales (NSW) and Australian Capital Territory (ACT)].
  • Have a bank account in good standing verifying the income deposits.
  • Have been on your current address for at least the last 3 months
  • Other requirements may apply
