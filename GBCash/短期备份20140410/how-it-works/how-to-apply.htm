How to apply?

Golden Bridge Cash SolutionTM makes the lending process as simple as possible for our clients. Click Apply Now , our easy online cash loan application process will guide you towards your quick cash. The lending progress is:
1. Read and complete our online application form.
2. Print, Sign and Fax the application along with the following documents to 1300 138 916
  . Latest pay slip stubs
  . Latest Bank account statement received in the last 30 days
  . Your Identification card (usually Driver's Licence or Passport)
  . A utility bill and mortgage documents or lease agreement. Each document to include current address and be current one month before you submit the loan application.
Remember you only need to do this ONCE.
3. Upon approval of your loan application, funds are directly deposited
into your account overnight.
4. Pay the installment when due by hassle-free direct debit from your
account on the agreed payment day.

The above 1&2 steps are for new customers ONLY. Once you successfully applied and repaid your first loan you will be eligible for members loan application. You can request a loan at any time by logging into the Members section.
