Payments:

How do I receive the cash, and how do I repay the loan?
We transfer money electronically. You pay us back by hassle-free direct debit from your account on the agreed payment day(s) . You don't need to worry about anything more than ensure you have enough money in your account on the due date. This service allows you to privately receive your loan without leaving your home.
What is the minimum amount I can pay towards my loan on my due date?
The minimum amount is simply the finance fees associated with your current principal balance. For example, if you have borrowed $100 for 15 days, your amount due is $120, the minimum amount you would be required to pay on your due date would be $20.
What if I can't pay the loan on the due date?
You may request an extension from member section on your short term loan or contact us immediately.
Extension option allows you to defer your installment payment until your next payday by paying an extension fee. Extension fee is the finance cost associated with your current principal balance.
If we do not receive an extension application or hear from you at least 2 business days before your loan is due, your payment will be considered late and your loan in default.
If an emergency arises and you are unable to repay your loan on the due date, we recommend the extension option to avoid late charges and remain in good standing with Golden Bridge Cash SolutionTM for future short term loan options.
